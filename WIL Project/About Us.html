About Us

Welcome to Empowering The Nation

We at Empowering the Nation believe that everyone should have the opportunity to flourish. Precious Radebe launched our business in 2018 with the goal of providing gardeners and domestic workers with the information and resources they need to advance their careers and embrace new opportunities.

What We Offer:

We offer a selection of courses that are tailored to our clients' specific requirements. We have a solution for everyone, whether you are an employer looking to increase the skills of your domestic employees or a job seeker looking to advance your career for better prospects. Among our programs: Short term: Life skills, child minding, cooking, Garden Maintenance. 6 Months: First Aid, Sewing, Landscaping
